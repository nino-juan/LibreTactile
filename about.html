Hi, we’re LibreTactile!


We bring together researchers, developers, users, and enthusiasts of tactile technology from various fields to promote equitable research, development, distribution, and support of open-source tactile technologies for human-computer interaction.

Join our efforts

A little about our history:


As part of my PhD research on equitable open-source HCI tech development, I lead a project to develop an assistive technology that helps visually impaired people navigate the internet. We faced a challenge in finding simple, affordable, and open-source tactile interfaces for our project, so we decided to create our own and make it open-source. This would enable more people to develop tactile interfaces for assistive technology, as well as other fields such as gaming, XR, VR neuro-rehab, etc. Given that this task required resources and expertise from different fields, we founded LibreTactile to bring together and orchestrate these efforts.

We believe in the power, flexibility, innovation, and resilience that diversity brings to communities. As such, we invite individuals from different backgrounds, nationalities, and interests, including those in assistive technology, user experience / rehabilitation research, consumer electronics, design, arts, XR, and gaming, to both benefit from and contribute to our mission.
